Force Diagrams


My first dataset that could be represented using a force diagram comes from a website that offers data on the historical transactions of Bitcoin. A force diagram could illuminate a summary of the transactions made. Each node would represent a party in the Bitcoin transaction and each edge would represent an actual exchange of Bitcoin. This force diagram would show who owns and the most Bitcoin and which owners are doing the most transactions. It would be insightful to the creators and owners of Bitcoin.


Bitcoin Transactions

My second dataset is full of profile data for Twitter users. This includes 40k users and the people they follow. I feel like this might infringe on privacy rights, but it would make a great force diagram! Each node could represent a user and each edge would represent a "friend" connection. It would illuminate who has the most "friends" and what kind of people they are "friending". It would be an interesting visualization of social dynamics.


Twitter Friends

My last dataset includes electoral donations in Brazil. It gives data on parties giving donations and parties recieving donations. Each node would represent a party and each edge would represent the donation. This force diagram would iluminate who is giving the most money in electoral donations and who is recieving it. This would be particularly useful to Brazil because their electoral system is infamous for corruption.
